PROJECTS

Py2700 Package

I developed the Py2700 package to interact with Keithley 2700 series digital multimeters using Python. The package provides an easy way to configure and read from the multimeter, incorporating saving to csv files for later analysis by default.

Learn More

RUSPS Cluster

I am the founder and director of the student-run Rutgers SPS cluster. The project aims to introduce undergraduates to high performance physics computing, and has acquired funding through a grant to provide multiple high-end GPUs for undergraduates to work on research projects involving machine learning.

Learn More

RAC Sound Study

I led a team of students to study the acoustics of the Rutgers Athletics Center and engage the community of Rutgers sports fans in an opportunity to learn more about how the physics of sound affects the Scarlet Knights' performance.
