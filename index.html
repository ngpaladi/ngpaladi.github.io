ABOUT ME

I am a physicist with a background in electrical and computer engineering. My primary research interests are experimental collider-based particle physics and computational high energy nuclear physics. I have worked on many projects both as part of and beyond my research. Outside of work, I enjoy both writing and playing music on the bass guitar as well as playing racquetball.
